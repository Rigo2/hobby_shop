GETTING STARTED

Getting into Gravel Riding or as also known, All road biking, can seem intemiating. In reality its super easy. All you need is a bike and dirt road, you don’t need a gravel specific bike just something comfortable and perferaably something with ticker tires. There isn’t much to gravel riding, take it easy and enjoy the scenery you find yourself in while exploring dirt roads or bike trails .

TIPS

Some of the best advice I’ve been givin while riding, is to always pack extra. Extra tubes, extra water, extra food and so on and so forth. It goes without saying you should always wear protective gear. I learned that the hard way a few years back after my cycling accident after I found myself needign thirteen stiches on my forehead from cliping a tree around a bend.

REALATED HOBBIES

Dirt biking and cycling are similar hobbies. going from cycling into dirtbiking is a good progression ans share similar motor functions.
